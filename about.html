The Story Behind the Consultancy

In the rapid evolution of 2025–2028, AI startups face a unique paradox: it has never been easier to build a prototype, yet never harder to build a repeatable business.

Generic startup playbooks ignore the specific infrastructure and data challenges unique to AI. We saw founders receiving plenty of theoretical advice but zero help on technical nuance, usage-based pricing experiments, or data strategy.

Tamara Pratt founded this consultancy to provide the fractional advisory that combines Go-To-Market strategy with the deep technical understanding required to scale LLM-driven products.
