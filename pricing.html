Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Custom Pricing" actually mean?

AI consultancy isn't a one-size-fits-all subscription. We price based on the scope of the project, whether it's a 2-week strategy sprint, a 3-month GTM build-out, or ongoing fractional advisory. We ensure the ROI always outweighs the investment.

Do you work with non-technical founders?

Yes. We specialize in helping non-technical founders bridge the gap between business vision and technical execution, ensuring you aren't over-building or choosing unworkable roadmaps.

How is this different from an incubator?

Incubators provide generic resources for many startups. Tamara Pratt provides deep, specific attention to your AI pricing experiments, technical stack, and repeatable sales motions—getting you to revenue faster than broad-spectrum programs.

When is the best time to hire a consultant?

Ideally, when you have a prototype or are planning your wedge. Hiring a consultant early prevents the "shiny demo" trap and ensures you're building for a real ICP from Day 1.
